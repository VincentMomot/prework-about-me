Bio:

Fall Out Boy is an American Rock Band formed in Wilmette, IL in 2001. Their two albums that made them popular were in 2005, From Under the Cork Tree, and in 2007, Infinity on High. After these albums, their concerts sold out completely and continued to produce 5 more albums. The group took a hiatus in 2009 due to burnout and returned in 2013.
